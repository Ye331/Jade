# ShanhaiGate Drop Wall Components

These sprites are made to continue the `Platform_To_Wall_*Drop` vertical join pieces.
They are visual skins only. Keep the graybox colliders as the gameplay source of truth.

## Sprite Set

- `DropWall_Extension_Tall.png`: long vertical wall extension below a platform drop.
- `DropWall_Extension_Short.png`: shorter wall extension.
- `DropWall_Bottom_Cap_Rooted.png`: bottom cap with roots and plants.
- `DropWall_Seam_Trim.png`: decorative band to hide horizontal joins between wall sections.
- `DropWall_Side_Filler_Left.png`: left exposed side filler.
- `DropWall_Side_Filler_Right.png`: right exposed side filler.

## Placement

- Put these behind or under the matching platform-to-wall connector.
- Overlap neighboring art by `0.1` to `0.3` Unity units so seams disappear.
- Main wall sorting order: `3` to `5`.
- Trim and side fillers sorting order: `6` to `9`.
